      Purely Adult Large Salmon & Beef

      27% PROTEIN, 14% FAT

      • Grain free recipe
      • Beef
      • Fish

      For adult dogs of larger sizes and breeds with a normal activity level.

      It contains tasty freshly prepared salmon  adding high levels of nutritional and natural fatty acids.  Produced with only two animal protein sources which makes it suitable for those sensitive to other animal proteins. The kibble size is suitable for larger breeds.

      • Grain free recipe.
      • Duo animal protein.
      • Freshly prepared salmon.
      • With arctic berries, a natural source of antioxidants.
      • With FOS, a prebiotic fibre that promotes good, functional gut bacteria.
      • With Taurine, to support a healthy heart.
        Sizes:         2,5 kg, 11 kg

      Ingredients

      COMPOSITION:

      Salmon 20% (freshly prepared salmon 14%, dried salmon protein 4%, hydrolysed salmon protein 2%), pea starch*, dried potato*, dried beef protein 16%, animal fat 7%, minerals, dried beet pulp*, vegetable oil, lignocellulose*, chicory inulin* (source of FOS 0.49%), rosehip meal* 0.25%, yeast*, dried cloudberries* 0.005%, dried crowberries* 0.005%. *Natural ingredients

      ADDITIVES (PER KG):

      Nutritional additives: Vitamin A 15 000IU, Vitamin D3 780IU, Vitamin E 100mg, taurine 1000mg, copper (copper(II)sulphate, pentahydrate) 5.8mg; manganese (manganese(II)oxide) 6.4mg; zinc (zinc sulphate, monohydrate) 36mg; iodine (calcium iodate, anhydrous) 1.7mg; selenium (selenium yeast) 0.1mg. Technical additives: Antioxidants.

      ANALYTICAL CONSTITUENTS:

      Protein 27%, fat content 14%, crude fibre 4%, crude ash 8% (of which calcium 1.2% and phosphorus 0.8%), omega 6 1,9%, omega 3 0.38%, moisture 9.5%. Metabolisable energy 1501kJ/100 g.

      Feeding guide

      WEIGHT OF DOG (KG) DAILY RATIO (G) DAILY RATION (DL)
      10-15 140-185 3.5-4.5
      20-25 230-275 6-7
      30-35 315-355 8-9
      40-50 390-460 9.5-11.5
      60-70 530-595 13-15

      Weight, age and activity level are factors that can affect your dogs energyneed. Therefore, see the feeding guidelines only as guidance. When changing food, gradually increase the new food over a period of 2-4 weeks. The dog should always have access to fresh water.

      Use: Complete feed for adult dogs of larger breeds with a normal activity level. Grainfree recipe. Can be softened in lukewarm/cold water or served dry together with a bowl of fresh water.

      Swedish Natural Quality embodies more than just the food in the bowl. It involves every part of our process, all the way from farms, forests or waters of Sweden to where and how we prepare the food.
